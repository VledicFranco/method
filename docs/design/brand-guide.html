01 — Foundation

Brand Narrative

The purpose, positioning, and worldview that make everything else legible. This is the "why" before the "what."

The Founding Thesis

Science and technology are not inherently good or harmful. They are instruments — and instruments amplify whoever wields them. The most important work of our time is ensuring they amplify human beings and the living world.

Positioning Statement

Vidtecci is a personal organization that builds tools, methods, and knowledge systems to guide the use of science and technology in service of human flourishing and ecological balance.

Brand Personality

Four words. Each defined by what it means and what it explicitly does not mean.

Precise
Specific, falsifiable, exact
Not pedantic, not cold
Principled
Conviction under pressure, coherent values
Not preachy, not rigid
Curious
Open-ended, genuinely interested, investigative
Not naive, not undirected
Warm
Human, honest, for-life energy
Not soft, not sentimental

The Door Sentence

The single sentence that introduces Vidtecci to any stranger in any context.

We build with science and technology, for life.

Specific enough to exclude. Broad enough to last.

05 — Communication

Voice & Tone

Voice is constant — it is who Vidtecci is. Tone is variable — it shifts by context. Both must be documented with concrete examples, not abstract adjectives.

Voice: Always

Vidtecci sounds like

Specific over vague. "We won't build systems that remove human judgment" — not "we value human agency."

Active over passive. "We evaluate every project against ecological impact" — not "ecological impact is evaluated."

Precise warmth. Curious, direct, for-life without being soft or sentimental.

Never sounds like

Corporate aspiration. "We are committed to excellence and innovation" — means nothing, excludes nothing.

Activist sloganeering. "Join the movement!" — too broad, too performed.

Academic hedging. "It could be argued that technology may potentially..." — say the claim.

Tone by Context

Publishing work / methods
Precise, evidence-forward, intellectually honest
"Phase 0 exit condition: one person producing, consistently, what would require an organization without AI. This is the measure — not hours saved, but organizational-scale output sustained."
Mission statements / public presence
Principled, clear, warmly ambitious
"We build with science and technology, for life. Not because it is easy — because it is what this work demands."
Documenting decisions / values
Direct, specific, defensible under pressure
"Violated by: fully automated systems with no appeal · tools that optimize human behavior rather than serve it. The punishment test: would we hold this value even if a competitor's product won the market by violating it?"

06 — Visual System

Visual Language

The aesthetic thesis: life at all scales. Biological precision seen at cosmic scope. Every visual element should feel like it belongs in a CBL album or an Aperture Science test chamber.

The Five Principles

01
Depth before decoration
Light emerges from within against darkness. Not surface color applied to a pale canvas — luminance from a dark ground.
02
Precision in service of wonder
Aperture's rigor is not cold — it reveals. Every element is placed exactly, in service of making living content more visible.
03
Organic geometry
Shapes are geometric; their arrangement is organic. Circles suggest cells. Grids breathe. Structure and life share a visual language.
04
Warm signal on cold cosmos
Solar Warmth and amber are the human signal — used as accent only. They mark the person, the emotion, the individual against the vast.

Cellular Network Motif

The primary Vidtecci visual motif: nodes connected by faint lines. Could be a microscope slide or a star map. Used in backgrounds, headers, and large-format surfaces.

Anti-Patterns

Anti-patternWhy it fails
Bright white backgroundErases the CBL void argument — life needs darkness to glow against
Generic startup paletteDefault aesthetic communicates nothing about Vidtecci's specific mission
Nature photography (landscapes)Too literal — CBL's aesthetic is microscopic, not scenic
Neon / cyberpunkBioluminescence is organic glow, not LED. Comes from within organisms, not power sources
Flat designDepth is structural to the thesis — void and glow require dimensionality
Solar Warmth as defaultWarmth marks the human specifically — using it everywhere neutralizes its meaning
07 — Visual System

Motion

Motion is purposeful and precise, never decorative. Default to productive motion. Reserve expressive motion for genuine narrative moments. Hover the demos to preview.

Easing Curves

Ease Out
cubic-bezier(0.16, 1, 0.3, 1)
Default for all UI entrances. Fast start, soft landing.
Circular
cubic-bezier(0.77, 0, 0.175, 1)
Wipe reveals, clip-path transitions. Crisp and intentional.

cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)
Expressive moments only — success states, key milestones.
Linear
linear
Continuous loops only — breathing glows, border beams, particle drift.

Duration Scale

TypeDurationExamples
Micro80–150msButton hover, color transitions, focus rings
Small200–300msTooltip appear, dropdown open, tag animations
Standard400–600msCard entrance, modal appear, page transitions
Expressive800–1200msHero wordmark, section reveals, counter animation
Ambient3000–10000msBreathing glows, border beams, particle network, SVG rings

Productive vs. Expressive

Productive — default

Fast, functional, gets out of the way. Button transitions, state changes, navigation. Never distracts from content. User barely notices it.

Expressive — selective

Slower, personality-bearing. Hero entrance, onboarding moments, major reveals. Used deliberately, not by default. Maximum 2–3 per page.
