# Vidtecci — Visual Identity Compass

**Author:** Orla (Visual & Aesthetic Architect)
**Version:** v1.0 — 2026-03-09
**Purpose:** A set of principles precise enough that any artifact can be checked against them and either passes or fails.

> *The universe taken apart with precision to understand what life is made of — and put back together in service of it.*

---

## The Visual Thesis

**Life at all scales.**

A Vidtecci artifact holds tension between the microscopic and the cosmic. A cell that looks like a galaxy. A data structure that breathes. The biological seen at the scope of the universe. This is the CBL argument made visual — and it is the same argument as the organizational thesis: carbon life is significant at every scale, from cellular to planetary.

## Typography

### Typeface Hierarchy

| Role | Typeface | Why |
|------|----------|-----|
| **Display / Headings** | [Space Grotesk](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Space+Grotesk) | Geometric precision with organic personality — clinical enough for Aperture, alive enough for CBL |
| **Body / Reading** | [Inter](https://fonts.google.com/specimen/Inter) | Maximum legibility, neutral enough to not fight with the palette |
| **Technical / Mono** | [JetBrains Mono](https://www.jetbrains.com/lp/mono/) | Code, data, annotations — precise, clear, scientific |

### Type Principles

- **Headings live on dark.** Primary display type is Stellar White on Deep Void or Abyssal Blue. Not black on white.
- **Scale is structural.** Heading hierarchy communicates information architecture, not style. No decorative large type.
- **Mono is equal.** Technical and code elements are not subordinated typographically. In a science and technology organization, data is primary content.
- **Generous spacing.** Line height 1.6+ for body. Space between sections. Void is not empty — it is breathing room.

## Visual Principles

Five principles. Each traces back to the identity thesis. Any artifact can be checked against each one.

### 1. Depth Before Decoration

Light emerges from within, against darkness. Not surface color applied to a pale canvas — luminance from a dark ground. The bioluminescent glow *means something*: life is luminous and the void it lives in is vast.

**Check:** Is the background dark? Does the light feel like it comes from within? If the artifact would look the same on a white background — it is not Vidtecci.

### 2. Precision in Service of Wonder

Aperture Science's clinical geometry is not cold — it is rigorous. Every element is placed exactly, every edge is sharp, every proportion is intentional. But the purpose of that precision is to reveal something about life, not to demonstrate control.

**Check:** Is every design decision justifiable? Does the precision serve the content or perform itself? If the rigorous structure doesn't make the living content more visible — simplify.

### 3. Organic Geometry

Shapes are geometric; their arrangement is organic. Circles suggest cells. Grids breathe. The Aperture grid and the CBL cellular structure are the same pattern at different scales. This is not metaphor — it is the visual argument that mathematical structure and biological life share the same underlying language.

**Check:** Does the composition feel like it could be a microscope image or a star map? Does the geometry have life in it — or is it purely mechanical?

### 4. Warm Signal on Cold Cosmos

The warm palette (Solar Warmth, Deep Amber) appears as accent against the cool deep palette. It marks the human. The person, the emotion, the warmth of individual agency — a signal against the vast and indifferent but beautiful universe.

**Check:** Is the warm accent being used for something genuinely human — a call to action, a person's name, a moment of emphasis that matters? Warmth is not generic emphasis. It marks the human dimension specifically.

### 5. Scale Without Hierarchy

Microscopic and cosmic are equally valid scales. A cell is not less significant than a galaxy. A single person is not less significant than an organization. Visual compositions should hold multiple scales simultaneously — zoomed-in texture in the same frame as expansive negative space.

**Check:** Does the composition exist at more than one scale? Does it honor both the detail and the vastness?

## Texture and Motif Language

### What Vidtecci images feel like
- **Microscopy aesthetic** — the visual grammar of electron microscopy and fluorescence imaging. Structures that could be cellular organelles or cosmic filaments.
- **Negative space as content** — large areas of Deep Void or Abyssal Blue are not empty. They are the universe the life exists in. Do not fill them.
- **Soft glow, hard edge** — bioluminescent elements have soft, diffuse glow. Structural elements (grids, lines, typography) have hard, precise edges. The two coexist in the same frame.
- **Grain** — a subtle film grain or noise texture connects the digital to the analog. Tycho's warmth. Prevents the clinical from becoming sterile.

### Motif library (to develop in Phase 1)
- Radial forms — cellular, stellar, both at once
- Network graphs — biological and computational networks share the same visual grammar
- Gradient spheres — planets, cells, light sources
- Fine grid overlays — the Aperture substrate

## Anti-Patterns

These are what Vidtecci artifacts must not be.

| Anti-pattern | Why it fails |
|-------------|-------------|
| **Bright white background with standard corporate colors** | Aperture's white is clinical precision on a dark ground. Corporate white is default — it communicates nothing. |
| **Generic startup sans-serif on light background** | This is the aesthetic of optimization and growth metrics. Vidtecci is not a startup optimizing for scale — it is an organization optimizing for life. |
| **Nature photography (landscapes, skies)** | Too literal. CBL's aesthetic is *microscopic* — life at the scale that most people never see. Not sunsets. |
| **Aggressive neon** | Bioluminescence is organic glow, not cyberpunk LED. The light comes from the organism, not from a power source. |
| **Flat design** | Depth is essential to the thesis. A flat palette denies the vastness of the void and the luminance of life within it. |
| **Warm as default** | Solar Warmth and amber are reserved for the human signal. Using them everywhere neutralizes their meaning. |
| **Decorative complexity** | Complexity that doesn't serve the content. The visual system is already rich — additional decoration competes with the signal. |

## Application Notes

**Documents and reports:** Dark background, Stellar White body text, Bioluminescent for section accents and active elements. Space Grotesk headings, Inter body.

**Diagrams and system maps:** Abyssal Blue ground, Deep Cellular or Nebular for inactive nodes, Bioluminescent for active/highlighted nodes, warm accents for human actors.

**Cover and title pages:** Full Deep Void background with a single gradient or organic motif. Display type in Stellar White. Minimal — the motif carries the weight.

**Code and technical content:** JetBrains Mono, Dim White on Abyssal Blue. Bioluminescent for syntax highlights.
