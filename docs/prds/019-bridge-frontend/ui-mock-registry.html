What
Persistent governance body that reviews priorities, steers project direction, and commissions agent work.
Who
The human operator and the methodology-aware agent. Council sessions require both roles.
Why
Without a deliberate steering mechanism, projects drift toward local optima. The council provides a recurring checkpoint where strategic direction is evaluated against the project's essence.
How
Structured session protocol: delta triage, agenda review, prioritization, commission generation.
When
At the start of each work period or when accumulated deltas exceed the triage threshold.

Axioms
ID Statement
Ax-1 Every delta must be triaged before agenda review begins.
Ax-2 Agenda items are prioritized relative to the project's essence.
Ax-3 A commission binds exactly one agenda item to one agent session.
Ax-4 The council cannot modify the essence — only guard it.
Ax-5 Stale agenda items (3+ sessions) must be resolved or archived.
Ax-6 Council decisions are recorded in the session log before execution.
Ax-7 Rejected deltas include rationale and are preserved in the log.
Ax-8 The council reviews retrospective observations for methodology feedback loops.
